# CV

Curriculum vitae of Sam Hillman: real-world evidence and AI scientist in oncology, with a PhD in ecology and evolutionary biology from the University of Edinburgh.

I’m a real-world evidence (RWE) and AI scientist with over five years’ experience generating regulatory-relevant evidence across the drug development lifecycle. My work combines observational study design and causal inference — target trial emulation, external control arms, comparative effectiveness — with the applied use of AI and machine learning to accelerate and standardise evidence generation. I’m based in Barcelona, Spain.
